Welcome to Honest Appalachia's documentation!
=============================================

..  image:: images/logo_small.png
    :align: center 

..  note::
    This documentation is under development.

Honest Appalachia is a secure website designed to help whistleblowers safely share corporate and government documents with journalists and the public.

..  warning::
    Being a whistleblower can be risky. Although it is impossible to guarantee that your identity will be protected, we do everything in our power to protect our sources.

Quick Links
-----------

Are you interested in submitting documents to us? Read the :ref:`online-submission` first.

Do you want to learn more about secure communication as an activist? Check out our guide to :ref:`secure-email`.

Do you want to hack on Honest Appalachia, or start your own whistleblower website focused on local/regional issues? Check out the :ref:`contribute` page.
